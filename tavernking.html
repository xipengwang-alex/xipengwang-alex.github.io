Tavern King



Join the tavern with your cards in hand along with closed fists. Fight through different opponents using your deck and your literal hands, while taking breaks at the bar. Become the “tavern king” after beating everyone

“Tavern king” is set in a fantasy bar where people duel each other with cards. The player's objective is to brawl different opponents in a single run using their wit and fists. In between each brawl, the player can stop by the tavern's bar to buy items that would help them complete their run. To win and become “Tavern King”, the player needs to beat every opponent they see and collect enough gold to purchase the Crown of Tavern King.

The goal of this game is to be enjoyable and strategic while also being humorous. It's complexity comes in the combination of cards but the actual difficulty is on the easier side of things. The tavern bar serves as the shop and makes the game easier for the player to complete their run. However, it can only be accessed after battles so how useful it is is all depending on the skill and wit of the player.
